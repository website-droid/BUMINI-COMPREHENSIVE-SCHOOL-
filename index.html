📜 Heritage & Growth

Founded in 1919 by Maryhill Missionaries, Bumini Comprehensive is the oldest school in Mumias East. Initially a primary institution, it has evolved into a full CBC comprehensive centre (Primary + Junior Secondary) serving over 1,600 students, with the Junior Secondary wing alone hosting 500+ learners. Sponsored by St. Luke's Catholic Church, the school has educated generations. The primary section hosts 885 pupils with 19 permanent classrooms, while JSS continues to expand rapidly. Under Head Teacher Mr. Issa Matala and Deputy Madam Everline, we've achieved #1 ranking.
